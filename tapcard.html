Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TapCards

What phones are compatible with TapCards?

All modern smartphones with NFC capability are compatible with TapCards. This includes:

  • All iPhones from iPhone 7 and newer (iOS 13+)
  • Most Android phones from the last 5 years
  • Recent Samsung, Google, OnePlus, and other major brands

If your phone can make contactless payments, it can read TapCards.

How do I change the link on my card?

Changing your card's destination is easy with our online dashboard:

  1. Log in to your TapCards account
  2. Select the card you want to update
  3. Enter the new URL or contact information
  4. Save your changes

The update takes effect immediately - no need to reprogram or replace your physical card.

What design options are available?

We offer complete customization for your TapCards:

Materials:

  • Premium plastic (standard)
  • Eco-friendly biodegradable
  • Metal composite (premium)

Finishes:

  • Matte or glossy
  • 3D textures and patterns
  • Debossed/embossed elements

Use our online designer or work with our design team for a completely custom look.

How durable are TapCards?

TapCards are designed for everyday use:

  • Water-resistant materials
  • Scratch-resistant coating
  • Tested for 10,000+ taps
  • Bend-resistant construction

While they're durable, we recommend treating them like you would a credit card - avoid extreme bending or exposure to harsh chemicals.

How long does shipping take?

Our standard production and shipping times:

  • Design approval: 1-2 business days
  • Production: 2-3 business days
  • Shipping within Nigeria: 1-3 business days
  • International shipping: 5-10 business days

Rush production and expedited shipping options are available for an additional fee.
